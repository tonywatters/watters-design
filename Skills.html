Skills Accelerator

The company needed to up-skill their workforce of nearly 60,000 Employees for future work. We created a Sharepoint website and developed multiple custom webparts that helped the employee view current skillsets and identify opportunities to upskill and expand their knowledge base.

This also proved useful to the company from an internal recuritment standpoint, utilizing pre existing skills sets and development in house talent.

Here are some of the designs and prototypes that was created.


Get Started card

The Get Started card was development to get the employee started on their skills journey and get them to build their eightfold profile.




Call to action cards

We created the Call to Action cards to help the employee gain entry to areas within the Skills application to build their profile, explore new roles, learning new skills, and book a career advice session with a Career Coach.




Skills cards

Skills announcements where created to keep employees informed about various events throughtout the company.




Jobs card

Available Jobs within the company, Employees can watch a video or apply for a specific job.

Career Advice booking system

We created a booking system to let Employees book meetings with a career coach.
