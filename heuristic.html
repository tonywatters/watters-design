Introduction

The company I work for needed me to do an Heuristic Evaluation on a existing product. After some research I saw some great ideas on dribbble where designers gain inspiration, feedback, community, but again none of the files where available for download, nevermind resuable XD templates.

I decided to create an Adobe XD Heuristic Evaluation template. The Heruistics are based on Jakob Nielsen's 10 general principles for interaction design. This template could then be shared with the entire UX team, A resuable XD template that could be used to evaluate any website or application if needed within the company.
